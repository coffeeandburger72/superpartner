## Love Language Strategy

### Her Ranking
1. **Quality Time (QT)** — Her longest, most enthusiastic messages are about shared experiences. "today was so fun, it's been so long since i've spent a whole day with someone and not gotten bored".
2. **Words of Affirmation (WA)** — Lights up when you compliment her creative work. "you really think it looks good? 🥹" Seeks validation but also gives freely.
3. **Receiving Gifts (RG)** — Excited by thoughtful (not expensive) gifts. "you remembered i said i wanted this!" The thought > the price.
4. **Acts of Service (AS)** — Appreciates unsolicited help but rarely asks. "you didn't have to, but thank you 🥹".
5. **Physical Touch (PT)** — References physical closeness in late-night messages. "i miss you" frequency increases during weeks without meeting.

### How to Apply This
- **Her #1 is QT** — The highest-impact investment is time, not money. Plan shared experiences: pottery, cafe hopping, photography walks, cooking together. Quality > quantity — one well-planned afternoon beats five rushed dinners.
- **Her #2 is WA** — Specific compliments hit harder than generic ones. "the curve on that logo is so smooth, you must've spent ages on it" >> "looks good". She needs to know you SEE her work, not just glance at it.
- **RG key insight** — "you remembered i said i wanted this..." appears multiple times. The gift itself matters less than the evidence that you listened weeks ago and acted on it. A $200 gift she mentioned once > a $1000 gift you guessed.
- **Don't neglect PT** — "i miss you" frequency spikes when you haven't met in a while. Physical presence refills her security tank. Plan regular in-person time, don't let weeks pass.
