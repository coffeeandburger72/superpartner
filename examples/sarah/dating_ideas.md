## Pro Tips

- **Confirm details early:** "so excited!! i'm thinking of wearing that new dress 🤩" — she engages MORE when plans have clear details (time, place, what to expect). Vague plans trigger her anxious side.
- **After-work dates are gold:** Her most talkative window is post-work evenings. A 7pm dinner after she's vented about clients = perfect timing.
- **Late night = vulnerability window:** "sometimes i feel like it's never good enough" comes out after 10pm. Don't plan activities — just be present.
- **Include Mochi stories:** Ask about her cat during dates. "what did Mochi do today" is a free mood booster.
- **Weekend mornings with Wing:** She has bi-weekly Saturday cafe dates with Wing. Don't schedule over these unless she offers — friendships are a core value.
- **Rain contingency:** She gets disproportionately deflated by weather changes. Have an indoor backup: "raining? how about pottery/cafe/exhibition"
- **Photography = bonding tool:** Bring her to photogenic spots and let her shoot. Watching her in her element + asking to see the shots = QT + WA.
- **The debrief message:** She ALWAYS sends a warm message within 30 min of getting home ("home! today was so fun 🥹"). Reply warmly — this is her attachment check-in. A cold or delayed response here will trigger her anxiety loop.
